New Zealand's Otherworldly Landscapes

If you flew south over these South Island alps and then carried on over the Pacific, your next sighting of land would be Antarctica. This view of the alps, from Blanket Bay lodge, was shared by some of the stars of the Lord of the Rings trilogy, who stayed here while filming.
